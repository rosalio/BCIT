COMP 1850 Final Project Abstract

The World Cup 2014 will be held in Brazil from June 13 to July 14. I'm planing to design and develop a website which includes the introduction of teams and players. The website should also contain details of each upcoming match such as time, location, and television broadcasting. I'm considering add more multimedia features such as match highlights.

The table below lists some of the contents for my World Cup 2014 website.

Pages Descriptions
Home Page Information of World Cup 2014
Fixtures and Results Lists of match fixtures and results
Standings Group standings and top scorers
Videos Embedded player for match highlights
Gallery display pictures
TV Broadcasting information of TV broadcasting by date
