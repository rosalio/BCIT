Welcome to my homepage! My name is Alex Dai. I was born in Shanghai, China and moved to Vancouver in 2008. After studying and working here for a few years, I really fell in love with this beautiful city. In December 2013, I became a landed immigrant.

I graduated from Simon Fraser University in December 2012. I am now working as a software development engineer at Amazon Vancouver in the logistics group. I am very interested in solving technical problems and discovering the unknown.

I have many hobbies. In my spare time, I go traveling and enjoy reading detective novels. One of my favorite is the Hercule Poirot series by Agatha Christie. I love sports. In particular, I am a big fan of soccer and a very big fan of Lionel Messi.
