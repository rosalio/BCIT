Arjen Robben

Arjen Robben is a Dutch footballer who plays for the German Bundesliga club Bayern Munich and the Netherlands national team. Robben has appeared at the 2004, 2008 and 2012 UEFA European Championships, and the 2006, 2010 and 2014 FIFA World Cups.

Born: January 23, 1984 (age 30), Bedum, Netherlands

Height: 1.80 m

Nationality: Dutch

Current teams: Netherlands national football team (#11 / Forward, Midfielder), FC Bayern Munich (#10 / Midfielder)
