About Me

Hello, welcome to my homepage! My name is Alex Dai. I was born in Shanghai, China and moved to Vancouver in 2008. After studying and working here for a few years, I really fell in love with this beautiful city. In December 2013, I became a permanent resident of Canada.

After graduating from Simon Fraser University with a M.A.Sc degree in 2013, I have been working as a software developer. I am really interested to solve technical problems and discover the unknown.

When I am not working, I love traveling and enjoy reading detective novels. One of my favorite is the Hercule Poirot series by Agatha Christie. I am also a big fan of soccer and a very big fan of Lionel Messi.
